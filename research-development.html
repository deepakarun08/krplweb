Research & Development

Spearheading the Roll Mill revolution in India with its inventive innovation based approach rooted in Kaizen, Kathuria has earned an enviable reputation and trust of its customers. Its success owes a lot to our Research and Development wing staffed by top-notch engineers and scientists. We have the equipment, the know-how and the will to power sophisticated engineering advances. These result in ease of use, durability, performance and energy efficiency in our machines and plants.

Our R&D division relies on its own researches and extensive tests as well as feedbacks from clients about performance of our equipment. Our teams constantly monitor machines on a periodic basis and interact with customer’s production engineers. Each such interaction and quality monitoring goes into our iterative and incremental improvement effort to empower us to achieve pinnacle of excellence. It reflects in our machinery and their performance.

R&D encompasses metallurgy, physical stress and endurance tests, engineering processes as well as the control and automation sections. We have sophisticated tools in each section. Each section works collaboratively in order to achieve coherence. This leads to better design, choice of best materials and processes within a certain cost framework but with no compromises. We plan and act for the future today.

We keep track of the latest technological developments in all related fields. We also research markets and customer preferences as well as competitors to know about sentiments and integrate all such intelligence into our comprehensive Research & Development policy. R&D, at KRPL touches everything we do.

Some of our latest innovations are:

  • Automatic Section Stacking Machine
  • Hydraulic Billet Shearing Machine
  • New Designed H,V Housingless Mill Stands
  • Convertible Mill Stands
  • Simply supported Online Section Straightening Machine
  • High Speed Pinch Roll (With speed upto 45 mtr./sec.)
  • Grip Tilter (For gripping, tilting & guiding the material)

& the QUEST continues……
