Online Section Straightening Machine

Simply supported ‘Online Section Straightening Machine’ is available with pneumatic combing roll system. This system is for accurate alignment of various sizes of angles, z section, channels and beams.

This start-stop machine has a special feature of Quick & Easy roll cartridge removal and placement. The machine is available both in manual and motorized pressure rolls adjustment system.
